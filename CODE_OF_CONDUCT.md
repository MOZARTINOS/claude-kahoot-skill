# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Demonstrating empathy and kindness toward other people
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and accepting constructive feedback
- Accepting responsibility, apologizing, and learning from mistakes
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing another person's private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior through a private GitHub security advisory:

https://github.com/MOZARTINOS/claude-kahoot-skill/security/advisories/new

Project maintainers will respect the privacy and security of reporters.
